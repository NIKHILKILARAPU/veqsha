Every Child Deserves Love

Every child comes into this world with innocent dreams and a heart full of hope. They deserve to grow up feeling loved, protected, and valued—no matter who they are or where they come from. Sadly, many children face struggles and loneliness at a very young age, missing the warmth of a family. At our orphanage, we believe that love has the power to heal and transform lives. We strive to create a joyful and caring environment where children can flourish emotionally, mentally, and physically. Through education, guidance, and the kindness of people like you, we help them believe in themselves again. When you extend your love and support, you give them not just comfort for today, but the courage to dream for tomorrow. Because every child deserves to feel special… every child deserves a chance… and most importantly, every child deserves love.

Truth Charitable, nestled in the heart of Pedagantyada, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, stands as a beacon of hope and compassion. As a non-profit organization, we are steadfast in our commitment to enhancing the social welfare of our beloved community. Through dedicated efforts and collaborative initiatives, we strive to uplift lives, foster empowerment, and create a brighter tomorrow for all.

  • Social security services for the elderly and the disabled people
  • Rehabilitation Services
  • Medical Services
  • Social Services
  • Family & Child Welfare Services
  • Employment Advisory Services to Young People

Our FOUNDER

P.Abhisekh

P. Abhishek, the esteemed founder and chairman of Truth Charitable Trust, is a visionary leader deeply committed to serving humanity. With a passion for social welfare and a heart dedicated to uplifting the disadvantaged, Abhishek embarked on a noble journey to establish the Trust, guided by the principles of compassion, integrity, and empathy.

Under his dynamic leadership, Truth Charitable Trust has flourished as a beacon of hope, catalyzing positive change and transforming countless lives. Abhishek’s visionary initiatives have touched the lives of the underprivileged, offering them solace, support, and opportunities for a brighter future.

Driven by a profound sense of duty towards society, Abhishek tirelessly champions the cause of the marginalized, spearheading impactful projects that address critical issues such as healthcare, education, poverty alleviation, and empowerment. His unwavering dedication, coupled with his visionary approach, continues to inspire others to join hands in making a meaningful difference in the world.

As a compassionate humanitarian and a visionary leader, P. Abhishek’s legacy at Truth Charitable Trust resonates with compassion, empathy, and unwavering dedication to serving humanity, enriching lives, and fostering a more equitable and compassionate world for all.
